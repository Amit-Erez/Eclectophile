Hey,
I'm EclectoPhile!

I'm a composer, arranger, producer, and audio engineer...but mostly, a life-long musical explorer with a deep love for sounds that don't stay in their lane. As the name suggests, I'm proudly eclectic.

My music blends analog warmth with digital finesse, acoustic soul with electronic sheen, and traditions from across the globe: from smoky 70s psychedelia to hypnotic Turkish rhythms, soulful jazz to Balkan grooves, Indian ragas to retro synths, and everything in between.

I found my groove as a keyboard player in bands during my teen years, and that's when composing original, instrumental music became my thing. At 20, I got serious: studied classical piano, music theory, then soundtrack composition, which opened the door to understanding the kind of artist I wanted to become.

It's taken a couple of decades (and a few thousand late-night studio sessions), but my personal music is finally stepping out into the world — raw, cinematic, borderless, and deeply human.

If you listen closely, you might find yourself standing in a dusty Middle Eastern festival in 1973… or wandering through an urban alley in modern-day London… or maybe somewhere entirely new.

Wherever it takes you — welcome to the journey!
