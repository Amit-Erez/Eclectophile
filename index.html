About EclectoPhile

EclectoPhile is the sonic identity of composer and producer Amir Khay Nir, whose musical journey spans nearly three decades. With roots in soundtrack composition, a foundation in music and audio technology, and a lifelong passion for sonic storytelling, EclectoPhile weaves together genres, eras, and emotional textures into a style that's entirely his own.
